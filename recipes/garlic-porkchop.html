Tender Juicy Garlic Porkchop ala Young Fresho

Ingredients

  • Brine Solution

    • 1 liter of Water
    • 1 tbsp of Salt
    • 1 tsp of Pepper Corn
    • 1 tsp of Coriander ❨Optional❩
    • 6 cloves of Garlic
    • 2 pcs of Dried Bay Leaves
    • 1 tsp of Soy Sauce

  • Marinade Solution

    • 1 tsp of Salt
    • 1 tsp of Pepper
    • 1 tsp of Washed or White Sugar
    • 1 tsp of Paprika ❨Optional❩
    • 1 tbsp of Soy Sauce
    • 1 tbsp of Cooking Oil
  • 4 pcs of Pork Chops
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

    Preparing the Brine Solution

    1. Pour 1 liter of Water into a mixing bowl.
    2. Add 1 tbsp of salt, 1 tsp of Pepper Corn, 1 tsp of Coriander ❨Optional❩, 6 cloves of Garlic, 2 pcs of Dried Bay Leaves and 1 tsp of Soy Sauce.
    3. Stir all ingredients well.
    4. Boil the brine solution for 2-5 minutes to render the flavors.
    5. Let it cool to room temperature.
    6. While waiting to cool, score the fat of the pork to prevent it from curling up when cooking.
    7. When the brine solution cools down to room temperature, pour the brine into the pork.
    8. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes. You may also let it sit overnight.
    9. After letting it sit in the brine solution, take out the pork chops and let it dry.

    Preparing the Marinade Solution

    1. On a separate container, mix 1 tsp of Salt, 1 tsp of Pepper, 1 tsp of Washed or White Sugar, 1 tsp of Paprika ❨Optional❩, 1 tbsp of Soy Sauce and 1 tbsp of Cooking Oil.
    2. Before applying the marinade solution, make sure to dry the pork chops with tissue.
    3. Apply the marinade solution by brushing it to the pork chops.
    4. Marinate it for at least 30 minutes.

    Frying the pork chops

    1. In a hot pan, pour cooking oil.
    2. When the cooking oil heats up, fry the pork chops in medium heat.
    3. Each side of the pork chops must be fried for about 3-5 minutes, or when the edges of the pork chops are nicely crisp.
